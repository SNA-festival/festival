Traditionally woven Welsh blankets and throws, scarves, handmade cushions and other gadgets. Fantastic Welsh gifts for the perfect present from Wales. Saint Dwynwen's Day is coming soon, 25th January each year, Wales' own Valentine's Day, traditionally a day when Welsh lovers give and receive cards and presents.

All our traditionally woven Welsh blankets, scarves, purses and handmade crafts are created in limited numbers with personal care and attention. None of our products will ever be exactly the same even if they have the same design, simply because they are handmade!

Our blankets, throws scarves or cushions suit all types of homes from modern apartments and city centre houses to more traditional homes in the countryside and our designs combine authentic Welsh tradition with an innovative and contemporary look. Our Welsh wool and UK rare-breed sheep wool is woven local mills in Wales on traditional looms using the skills and processes of yesteryear. We aim to source our woollen fabrics from these remaining mills not only because they are local but also to support the Welsh woollen industry.

All our Welsh blankets and throws are traditionally woven in Wales. We design and make handmade cushions, throws, bedding and other soft furnishings. We also design Welsh blankets which are woven for us in local mills. We use the fabrics from these local mills to make our handmade wool crafts. We use wherever possible, wool from quality rare-breed sheep. The origin of our raw materials is important to us. Some wool comes from the sheep on the farms that surround us. We do like to know our sheep!!!

So, why not treat yourself, your friends or loved ones to a beautiful, 100% wool Welsh blanket, throw or scarf... perfect Welsh Gifts for any occasion and St Dwynwen's Day - 25 January 2015.
